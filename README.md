## Project Overview

The idea is to use the Spiral programming language to rewrite Azeroth without the Maelstrom, essentially reimagining the very fabric of the World of Warcraft universe. This new iteration, simply titled "Azeroth", aims to create a vibrant and dynamic world where the players themselves can participate in shaping the narrative through decentralized governance and artificial intelligence mediation. By leveraging the robust capabilities of Rust and WebAssembly, this project will push the boundaries of what an MMORPG can be, intertwining advanced technology with rich storytelling and deep player engagement.

## Technological Backbone

**Spiral Programming Language:**  
Spiral, inspired by F# and designed for concurrent and safe system-level programming, is pivotal to this project. By compiling to Rust and Gleam, Spiral ensures both performance and safety, essential for building a complex, real-time MMORPG. Its syntax and capabilities allow for writing expressive and maintainable code, crucial for the ongoing development and scaling of the game.

**Rust and WebAssembly:**  
Utilizing Rust and WebAssembly guarantees a high-performance, secure, and cross-platform experience. Rust's memory safety features and WebAssembly's ability to run at near-native speed in web browsers make them the ideal choices for creating a seamless and immersive MMORPG experience.

**Artificial Intelligence:**  
AI will be central in the dynamic development of Azeroth. By learning from the existing gameplay of WoW servers, such as MaNGOS and Trinity, the AI will assist in generating quests, balancing gameplay mechanics, and personalizing player experiences. This AI-driven approach ensures the game remains engaging and challenging, adapting to the evolving skills and interests of its player base.

**Troll Empires:**  
Without the devastating split of the continents, the Troll empires, particularly the Zandalari, maintain their power and influence. These empires continue to evolve, with intricate political dynamics and a rich cultural heritage that deeply influences the world around them. Players will find themselves navigating a world where the Trolls are not just remnants of a bygone era but powerful players on the global stage.

**Azshara Unchanged:**  
Azshara, untransformed into the Naga, retains her elven form and formidable power. She remains a central figure, her ambitions driving many conflicts and alliances. Players will encounter her in numerous story arcs, from the grandiose courts of the Kaldorei to the hidden enclaves of her most loyal adherents, each encounter shedding more light on her ultimate goals.

**Human Kingdoms:**  
The human kingdoms experience a different trajectory, more interconnected with other races and cultures. This shared history fosters a rich tapestry of human civilization, with unique collaborative innovations and cultural exchanges. The humans' technological and magical advancements are more synchronized with those of other races, leading to a multifaceted narrative where players explore complex interdependencies and rivalries.

**Elemental Forces:**  
In this new Azeroth, elemental forces are evenly distributed, allowing for more harmonious interactions.
